Our Story

Biffany's was born from the simple, all American Dream of crushing another man's dream. Unfortunately, that loser scientist and his meddling kid ruined that dream. So, after failing in that attempt, our founder Biff Tannen, looked for another way to push forward, and found work at a nearby restaurant.

The smell of manure was pretty strong at first, so he started in the very back of the restaurant, but worked his way forward, always quoting his mantra "You can do anything if you put your mind to it." Turns out, Biff is a pretty good cook, if we say so ourselves! Over 50 years later, and Biffany's is still going strong! Why don't you make like a tree, and get in here too!
